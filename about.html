About Naqash

Naqash is a Middle Eastern discussion platform inspired by Reddit. It allows users to share ideas, ask questions, and engage in communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an open platform for Arabic users to communicate, share knowledge, and build communities.

Key Features

  • Create and share posts
  • Vote on content
  • Join communities
  • Comment and interact
  • User profiles and dashboards

Help & FAQ

How do I create a post?

Go to "Create Post" and submit your content.

Do I need an account?

Yes, you must register to interact.
